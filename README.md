# 🌱 NavJeevan – A Digital Lifeline for Rural India

> Empowering rural communities through accessible and impactful solutions in agriculture, healthcare, education, and skill development.

## 📖 Overview

NavJeevan is an all-in-one digital platform built to improve the lives of people in rural India by providing essential services through technology. The platform connects users with reliable agricultural information, healthcare assistance, educational resources, government welfare schemes, employment opportunities, and AI-powered support. With multilingual accessibility and an intelligent chatbot, NavJeevan ensures that digital services are simple, inclusive, and accessible for everyone.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🌾 Agriculture
- Live crop market prices
- Weather forecasts
- Crop advisory and best farming practices
- Government agriculture schemes
- Fertilizer and pesticide recommendations

### 🏥 Healthcare
- Health awareness and wellness tips
- Nearby hospitals and healthcare centers
- Emergency contacts
- Online health guidance

### 📚 Education
- Free study materials
- Scholarships and government schemes
- Career guidance
- Digital literacy resources

### 💼 Skill Development
- Job opportunities
- Vocational training programs
- Entrepreneurship support
- Resume and interview guidance

### 🤖 AI Chatbot
- 24×7 AI-powered assistance
- Answers agriculture-related queries
- Healthcare guidance
- Government scheme information

- Easy-to-use conversational interface

### 🌐 Multi-Language Support
- Supports multiple Indian languages
